ABOUT

The Brass Brunch Bunch is a daily FREE social gathering of brass players to work on a daily routine. We have started this group on March 9th 2020 and have kept going ever since. It keeps us practicing and it helps us network authentically with each other via video.

Make Friends, Learn, and Share!

Music is on screen via ZOOM. The event is free to participate internationally.  All age and ability levels, we will breakout into groups once we get large.  Guest musicians from around the world.

Questions - Answers (FAQ for the event)

 I am in Middle School or High School.  Is this the right place for me?

Absolutely!  Make sure your parents are around, but this group is designed for all ability levels, as long as you want to practice and get better

What instruments is this group designed for?

We have spent the last 3 months focusing on Tuba, Euphonium and Trombone. We have just changed our music over to include Trumpet and Horn (Eb or F)  

Do you need to download any software?

If you are on a computer, you can join us from the browser.  If you are on a mobile device you will need to download Zoom.

How do you have 100 people play together online?

We eather use a call and responce system with 2 group members, or we have a member lead a different exercise.  Join us and see how easy it is!
